We build calm, dependable software that helps people stay on top of property ownership and rental-property operations — without the clutter. Our current app, HaliteHomes, is a local-first home maintenance and property records app.

What we focus on

  • Property ownership — appliances, repairs, warranties, documents, and a maintenance schedule that doesn’t get forgotten.
  • Rental-property operations — tenant handoffs, move-in/out records, and per-property organization for small landlords.
  • Local-first organization — your records stay on your device unless you choose to export them.
