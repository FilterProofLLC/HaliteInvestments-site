A Halite Investments app

HaliteHomes

A local-first home maintenance and property records app for tenants, homeowners, and small landlords.

Coming soon on Google Play

HaliteHomes helps reduce forgotten home-maintenance and property-handoff information. It keeps the records a property actually needs — organized, private, and on your device.

Built for three kinds of people

Tenant

Free, simple home records: basic maintenance, smart-home/technology handoff info, notes, and move-in/out support for the place you rent.

Homeowner

Full single-home upkeep: appliance records, repairs & receipts, documents, insurance/warranty info, a yearly maintenance schedule, and PDF exports.

Landlord / small property manager

Multi-property records, tenant handoff tools, technology sheets, move-in/out documents, repairs/receipts, and portfolio organization.

What you can track

  • Maintenance schedules and reminders
  • Appliances (model, serial, warranty, filter info)
  • Property/home profile records
  • Repairs and receipts
  • Insurance and home warranty records
  • Property notes and documents
  • Smart-home / technology device sheets
  • Move-in / move-out checklist
  • Home information PDF exports
  • Annual maintenance schedule PDF exports
  • Encrypted local backup / export

Private by design

  • Local-first — records live on your device.
  • No account required — nothing to sign up for.
  • No cloud sync currently — your property data stays on the device unless you choose to export it.
  • You can create PDFs or an encrypted backup and share/save them yourself.

Purchases

HaliteHomes is free to start. Optional in-app purchases may unlock Homeowner Pro, Landlord Pro, and the PDF export tools. On Android, purchases are handled through Google Play. Property data is never sent to the purchase provider.
