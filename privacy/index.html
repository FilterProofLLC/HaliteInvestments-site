Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how HaliteHomes, operated by Halite Investments LLC (“we,” “us”), handles your information. HaliteHomes is a local-first app: it is designed to keep your data on your device.

The short version

  • HaliteHomes is local-first — your data stays on your device.
  • HaliteHomes does not create user accounts.
  • HaliteHomes does not upload your home/property data to Halite Investments servers.
  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not share your data with advertisers.
  • HaliteHomes does not use ads, analytics, or tracking at this time.
  • Google Play may process purchase information when you interact with in-app purchases.
  • If you voluntarily email feedback or support, that email is received by Halite Investments at Haliteinvestments@gmail.com.

What data the app stores

Information you enter is stored locally on your device, including home and property records, property profile details, maintenance tasks and schedules, appliances, repairs and receipts, insurance and warranty records, notes, document references, tenant-handoff records, move-in/move-out checklist data, technology/device records and any smart-home or device access credentials you add, and encrypted backup data you create. We do not receive or store this information on our servers, and HaliteHomes does not upload your home/property data to Halite Investments servers — we do not operate a server for your app data.

Exports and backups

You may create PDFs or an encrypted backup file and choose to share or save them outside the app (for example, to your files, email, or another app). Once you share an exported PDF or backup outside HaliteHomes, that file is handled by you and by the receiving app or service, under their terms — not this policy. Keep backup passphrases safe; a forgotten passphrase cannot be recovered, and exported files that contain device passwords/access codes should only be shared with people authorized to access the property’s devices.

Purchases (Google Play Billing)

On Android, HaliteHomes may use Google Play Billing to offer optional in-app purchases. If you make a purchase, Google Play may process purchase information under Google’s own policies. HaliteHomes does not send your property records, tenant information, document contents, smart-home/device passwords, or backup contents to Google Play Billing. The billing process only involves purchase/entitlement information, not your property data.

Feedback and support

If you voluntarily contact us by email — for example, to report a bug or send feedback — that email is received by Halite Investments LLC at Haliteinvestments@gmail.com. Feedback emails should not include private home, property, or tenant data unless you intentionally choose to include it. Any correspondence you send is kept only to help us respond and improve the app.

Deleting your data

HaliteHomes stores app data locally on your device. You can delete local HaliteHomes data from inside the app by opening Settings, expanding App data & encrypted backup, and selecting Delete all local data. You can also remove locally stored app data by uninstalling HaliteHomes or clearing the app’s storage through Android settings.

Because HaliteHomes does not create user accounts or store home/property data on Halite Investments servers, there is no server-side account deletion process for home/property data. If you voluntarily contacted support by email and want that support correspondence deleted, contact Haliteinvestments@gmail.com.

Permissions

The app may request device permissions you initiate — for example, to take or choose a photo for a record, or to open the share sheet to export a file. These are used only for the action you start.

Children

HaliteHomes is intended for general property and home management and is not directed to children.

Security

We design HaliteHomes to keep data on your device and to encrypt backup exports with a passphrase you choose. No app or method of storage is completely secure, so we cannot promise absolute security. Protecting your device and your exported files is an important part of keeping your information private.

Future features

If we add features such as cloud sync or online lookup in the future, we will update this policy before those features are released.

Contact

Questions about this policy: Haliteinvestments@gmail.com.
